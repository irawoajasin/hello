2026

Worked on a couple of branding projects, published some writing on Data Intimacy to a zine collective dreaming of radical design futures. With my good friend Alissa, I co-designed and hosted a series of cyanotype workshop that used slow image capture as a site for inquiry in partnership with Telepathy, Open Data Week, and LowPwrLab. I also presented my datafied pasts, desirous futures installation at the Black Futures exhibition. Aand I tabled at the Black Zine Fair. Lastly I worked on my thesis!

2025

Did research with Ahmed Ansari on decolonial design of surveillance technologies. Tabled at the Jersey Art Book Fair and New York Zine Fair. Also worked with labs for liberation to develop glitch toolkits for a few community organizations in Atlanta (my hometown). I developed branding for a speaker series on fridge art practices and produced/presented my own art installation that had participants dial a cloud to read poetry on cloud computing mediation.

2024

I started grad school where I explored a lot on creative computing and wrote poetry! My main thing was for the illegible which was an experimental research project that contextualized a poetry zine. I also worked on a web-based installation called Embrace that troubled normative social network protocols. I also worked with a project supported by AIAI developing a booklet that introduced resellers to feminist, decolonial, and critical approaches to technology and labor.
